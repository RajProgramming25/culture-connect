Foods

African Foods

Fufu

A starchy staple made from cassava, yams, or plantains. It is pounded into a smooth, elastic dough and served with soups.

Bobotie

A South African dish made from minced meat (often beef or lamb) mixed with spices, fruit, and a custard topping.

Couscous

Steamed semolina granules often served with a stew or sauce made from meat, vegetables, and spices.

Injera

A sourdough flatbread with a spongy texture, made from teff flour.
