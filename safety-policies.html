Safety Policies

Introduction

At Culture Connect, the safety and well-being of our participants, staff, and volunteers are our top priorities. This page outlines our safety policies and procedures to ensure a secure and positive environment for everyone involved.

Emergency Procedures

Emergency Contacts

In case of emergency, please contact local emergency services immediately. Our on-site emergency contact numbers are:

  • Emergency Contact 1: (123) 456-7890
  • Emergency Contact 2: (123) 456-7891

Evacuation Plan

In the event of an evacuation, follow these steps:

  1. Remain calm and listen to instructions from staff members.
  2. Proceed to the nearest exit as indicated by evacuation signs.
  3. Assemble at the designated assembly point outside the building.
  4. Do not re-enter the building until it is deemed safe by emergency personnel.

Health and Safety Guidelines

Hygiene

To maintain a clean and healthy environment, we ask everyone to:

  • Wash hands regularly with soap and water.
  • Use hand sanitizer where soap is not available.
  • Cover coughs and sneezes with a tissue or elbow.
  • Dispose of tissues properly and wash hands immediately.

COVID-19 Precautions

In response to the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic, we have implemented the following precautions:

  • Facemasks are required in indoor spaces where social distancing cannot be maintained.
  • Social distancing guidelines must be followed at all times.
  • Temperature checks may be conducted upon entry to our events.
  • Stay home if you are feeling unwell or have been exposed to someone with COVID-19.

Event Safety

General Guidelines

For the safety of all participants, please adhere to the following guidelines during events:

  • Follow all instructions provided by event staff and volunteers.
  • Report any hazards or unsafe conditions to staff immediately.
  • Keep personal belongings secure and avoid leaving items unattended.
  • In case of an accident or injury, notify a staff member right away.

First Aid

First aid kits are available at key locations throughout our event spaces. Trained first aid personnel are on-site during all major events to assist with any medical needs.

Code of Conduct

All participants, staff, and volunteers are expected to adhere to our Code of Conduct, which includes:

  • Respecting others and their cultural backgrounds.
  • Maintaining a positive and supportive environment.
  • Abiding by all safety regulations and policies.
  • Reporting any violations of safety policies or code of conduct.

Failure to comply with these policies may result in removal from the event or other disciplinary actions.
